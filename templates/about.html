Where artificial intelligence meets excellence

About the Application

Luxe Glow is an AI-powered skincare analysis system designed to understand and evaluate skin conditions through advanced computer vision and machine learning techniques.

By analyzing a facial image, the system can identify characteristics such as acne, pigmentation, redness, skin age and skin type. The goal is to provide intelligent insights that help individuals better understand their skin health.

Users can also get outfit recommendation based on their skin tone. The skin tone is calculated through MONK SKIN TONE SCALE (MST) which divides the skin tones into 10 categories.

This project demonstrates how artificial intelligence can be integrated with skincare technology to build smart and personalized beauty solutions.

Currently this is an MVP(Minimum Viable Product).
